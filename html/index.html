ABOUT US

Why Did We Start ?

21 years back rehabilitation in India was meant only for Alcholcalic or Drug addict patients. The concept of neuroortho rehab was very poor in India, few indoor integrated rehab center was in CMC Vellore, NIMHANS, Bangalore, NIOH Kolkata only. Though this medical science was very popular in the USA and other few countries, this new concept was not at all popular among the medical professionals as well as general People.

Dr. Ghatak a young Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation expert with the sharp vision of realizing the increasing enormity of the problems faced by a person inflicted with stroke, brain or spinal injuries, or early life problems like Cerebral Palsy decided to make a difference...
