Why Did We Start ?

Last updated on May 11th, 2022 by MRC

21 years back rehabilitation in India was meant only for Alcholcalic or Drug addict patients. The concept of neuroortho rehab was very poor in India, few indoor integrated rehab center was in CMC Vellore, NIMHANS, Bangalore, NIOH Kolkata only. Though this medical science was very popular in the USA and other few countries, this new concept was not at all popular among the medical professionals as well as general People.

Dr. Ghatak a young Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation expert with the sharp vision of realizing the increasing enormity of the problems faced by a person inflicted with stroke, brain or spinal injuries, or early life problems like Cerebral Palsy decided to make a difference.More over the number of such distress population was increasing day by day through out india and the eastern india & West Bengal circle was showing the same picture strikingly.

With His Iron Determination, He Took up The Challenge of Touching The Lives of Such Patients and Bring Back The Smile on Their Suffering Faces.

But it was not an easy start!

Dr. Ghatak was almost penniless and had to let go off various lucrative opportunities and work hard to achieve his goal. After months of struggle and hard work, he finally achieved his dream and started a full-fledged indoor rehabilitation center on this day(13th Sept) 21 years ago. During its initial years MRC had to fight a lot to prove itself by proving the efficacy of such rehab treatment by showing huge improvement of almost every patient who was told as "no more can be done" and they had to spend the rest of their life at a bedridden status. MRC was a ray of sunshine for such patients. Subsequently it became a boon for patients with disability never thought that they would be able to lead a normal life or come out of bed.
